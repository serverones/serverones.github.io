Question and Answer: The Ultimate Guide to Car Insurance

Q: What factors affect car insurance rates? A: Car insurance rates are determined by several factors, including your driving record, the type of car you drive, your age, and your location.

Q: Can I change my car insurance policy? A: Yes, you can change your car insurance policy at any time. However, you may be subject to cancellation fees or other penalties.

Q: Do I need car insurance if I don't own a car? A: If you frequently borrow or rent cars, it is a good idea to have non-owner car insurance to protect yourself in case of an accident.

Q: What should I do if I get into a car accident? A: If you are involved in a car accident, you should exchange information with the other driver, take photos of the scene, and report the incident to your insurance company.
